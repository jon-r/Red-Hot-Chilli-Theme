    Red Hot Chilli are experts in quality used catering equipment. We specialise in refurbishing heavy duty stainless steel appliances for commercial kitchens, but also deal in furnishings and smaller scale equipment. Over the years we have served a wide range of catering and hospitality companies, from fresh start ups, nationwide pub chains, and even catering props for television studios.

    From our showroom in Warrington, Cheshire, we regularly buy and sell used catering equipment locally to Manchester, Liverpool and North Wales. Our staff is happy to arrange transport nationwide, and even export to Europe.
